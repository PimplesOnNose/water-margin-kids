The Marshes of Liangshan

梁山泊

Long ago in China, there was a place called Liangshan Marsh. It was a wild, watery place where mountains met the sky.

Here, 108 heroes gathered. They came from all over China. Some were strong warriors. Some were clever scholars. All of them wanted to help the people.

很久以前，在中国有一个地方叫梁山泊。那是一个山水相依、烟波浩渺的地方。

Hěn jiǔ yǐqián, zài Zhōngguó yǒu yīgè dìfāng jiào Liángshān Pō. Nà shì yīgè shānshuǐ xiāngyī, yānbō hàomiǎo de dìfāng.

在这里，一百零八位好汉聚集。他们来自中国各地。有些是强壮的战士。有些是聪明的学者。他们都想帮助老百姓。

Zài zhèlǐ, yībǎi líng bā wèi hǎohàn jíjù. Tāmen lái zì Zhōngguó gèdì. Yǒuxiē shì qiángzhuàng de zhànshì. Yǒuxiē shì cōngmíng de xuézhě. Tāmen dōu xiǎng bāngzhù lǎobǎixìng.

Wu Song and the Tiger

武松打虎

One day, a brave man named Wu Song was walking through Jingyang Ridge. He was tall and strong.

Suddenly, a huge tiger jumped out! Wu Song was scared, but he didn't run. He fought the tiger with his bare hands and won! Everyone called him a hero.

有一天，一个名叫武松的勇敢男子正穿过景阳冈。他高大强壮。

Yǒu yītiān, yīgè míng jiào Wǔ Sōng de yǒnggǎn nánzǐ zhèng chuānguò Jǐngyáng Gāng. Tā gāodà qiángzhuàng.

突然，一只大老虎跳了出来！武松虽然害怕，但他没有逃跑。他赤手空拳与老虎搏斗并获胜！每个人都称他为英雄。

Tūrán, yī zhī dà lǎohǔ tiào le chūlái! Wǔ Sōng suīrán hàipà, dàn tā méiyǒu táopǎo. Tā chìshǒu kōngquán yǔ lǎohǔ bódòu bìng huòshèng! Měi gèrén dōu chēng tā wèi yīngxióng.

Lu Zhishen and the Willow Tree

鲁智深倒拔垂杨柳

Lu Zhishen was a monk with a big heart and even bigger muscles. He didn't like bullies.

One day, he saw a group of bullies bothering villagers. To show his strength, he grabbed a huge willow tree and pulled it right out of the ground! The bullies ran away scared.

鲁智深是一个心地善良、力大无穷的和尚。他最看不惯欺负人的坏蛋。

Lǔ Zhìshēn shì yīgè xīndì shànliáng, lìdà wúqióng de héshang. Tā zuì kàn bù guàn qīfu rén de huàidàn.

有一天，他看到一群恶霸在欺负村民。为了展示自己的力气，他抓住一棵大柳树，一下子把它从地里拔了出来！那些恶霸吓得赶紧逃跑了。

Yǒu yītiān, tā kàndào yī qún èbà zài qīfu cūnmín. Wèile zhǎnshì zìjǐ de lìqì, tā zhuāzhù yī kē dà liǔshù, yīxiàzi bǎ tā cóng dì lǐ bá le chūlái! Nàxiē èbà xià de gǎnjǐn táopǎo le.

Lin Chong's Journey

林冲发配

Lin Chong was a great fighter who worked for the emperor. But some bad people wanted to hurt him.

They sent him far away on a dangerous journey. Along the way, he had to fight many enemies. But his courage never stopped. He finally joined the heroes at Liangshan.

林冲是一位武艺高强的将领，为朝廷效力。但是一些坏人想要陷害他。

Lín Chōng shì yī wèi wǔyì gāoqiáng de jiànglǐng, wèi cháotíng xiàolì. Dànshì yīxiē huàirén xiǎng yào xiànhài tā.

他们把他发配到很远的地方，路途非常危险。一路上，他不得不与许多敌人战斗。但他的勇气从未停止。他最终加入了梁山的好汉们。

Tāmen bǎ tā fāpèi dào hěn yuǎn de dìfāng, lútú fēicháng wēixiǎn. Yīlù shàng, tā bùdébù yǔ xǔduō dírén zhàndòu. Dàn tā de yǒngqì cóngwèi tíngzhǐ. Tā zuìzhōng jiārù le Liángshān de hǎohànmen.

Li Kui's Two Axes

李逵双斧

Li Kui was known for his two big axes. He was very loyal to his friends.

He would do anything to protect them. Some people thought he was fierce, but he had a kind heart. He always helped the weak and fought the strong.

李逵以他的两把大板斧而闻名。他对朋友非常忠心。

Lǐ Kuí yǐ tā de liǎng bǎ dà bǎnfǔ ér wénmíng. Tā duì péngyǒu fēicháng zhōngxīn.

他愿意做任何事情来保护朋友。有些人觉得他很凶猛，但他有一颗善良的心。他总是帮助弱小，对抗强敌。

Tā yuànyì zuò rènhé shìqíng lái bǎohù péngyǒu. Yǒu xiē rén juédé tā hěn xiōngměng, dàn tā yǒu yī kē shànliáng de xīn. Tā zǒngshì bāngzhù ruòxiǎo, duìkàng qiángdí.

The Heroes' Feast

英雄宴会

All 108 heroes gathered at Liangshan for a big feast. They ate delicious food and drank tea.

They made a promise to always help each other and fight for justice. They became like one big family.

一百零八位好汉齐聚梁山，举行盛大的宴会。他们品尝美味佳肴，畅饮美酒。

Yībǎi líng bā wèi hǎohàn qíjù Liángshān, jǔxíng shèngdà de yànhuì. Tāmen pǐncháng měiwèi jiāyáo, chàngyǐn měijiǔ.

他们立下誓言，要互相帮助，伸张正义。他们就像一个大家庭一样团结。

Tāmen lìxià shìyán, yào hùxiāng bāngzhù, shēnzhāng zhèngyì. Tāmen jiù xiàng yīgè dà jiātíng yīyàng tuánjié.

Defending Liangshan

保卫梁山

The emperor sent a great army to attack Liangshan and capture the heroes. But the heroes were ready.

They used clever strategies and fought bravely together. Wave after wave of soldiers came, but the heroes would not give up. Their unity and courage drove the enemy away.

皇帝派了大军前来攻打梁山，想要捉拿好汉们。好汉们早已做好准备。

Huángdì pài le dàjūn qiánlái gōngdǎ Liángshān, xiǎng yào zhuōná hǎohànmen. Hǎohànmen zǎoyǐ zuòhǎo zhǔnbèi.

他们运用巧妙的计策，英勇奋战。一批又一批的官兵涌来，但好汉们团结一心，绝不放弃。他们的勇气最终击退了敌人。

Tāmen yùnyòng qiǎomiào de jìcè, yīngyǒng fènzhàn. Yī pī yòu yī pī de guānbīng yǒng lái, dàn hǎohànmen tuánjié yīxīn, jué bù fàngqì. Tāmen de yǒngqì zuìzhōng jītuì le dírén.

The Heroes' Code

英雄准则

The heroes lived by a special code. They promised to be brave, honest, and kind.

They helped the poor and stood up against injustice. Even though they were different, they all believed in doing the right thing.

好汉们遵循着一套特殊的准则。他们承诺要勇敢、诚实、善良。

Hǎohànmen zūnxúnzhe yī tào tèshū de zhǔnzé. Tāmen chéngnuò yào yǒnggǎn, chéngshí, shànliáng.

他们帮助穷人，反抗不公正的事情。虽然每个人都不一样，但他们都相信要做正确的事。

Tāmen bāngzhù qióngrén, fǎnkàng bù gōngzhèng de shìqíng. Suīrán měi gèrén dōu bù yīyàng, dàn tāmen dōu xiāngxìn yào zuò zhèngquè de shì.

The Legend Lives On

传说永存

Even today, people still tell stories about the 108 heroes of Liangshan.

Their bravery and friendship inspire us all. They show us that when we work together, we can overcome any challenge.

直到今天，人们仍然在传颂梁山一百零八位好汉的故事。

Zhídào jīntiān, rénmen réngrán zài chuánsòng Liángshān yībǎi líng bā wèi hǎohàn de gùshì.

他们的勇敢和友谊激励着我们每一个人。他们告诉我们，只要团结一致，就能克服任何困难。

Tāmen de yǒnggǎn hé yǒuyì jīlì zhe wǒmen měi yī gè rén. Tāmen gàosù wǒmen, zhǐyào tuánjié yīzhì, jiù néng kèfú rènhé kùnnán.

结束

感谢你阅读水浒英雄的故事！

Gǎnxiè nǐ yuèdú Shuǐhǔ yīngxióng de gùshì!

记住，只要你勇敢、善良、乐于助人，你也可以成为英雄！
